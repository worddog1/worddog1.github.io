Who We Are

We’re Luke Boman and Noah Letawsky, proud Sherwood Park locals.

We started this company three years ago with one goal: to work for ourselves, grow something we could be proud of, and serve the community we’ve called home our entire lives.

What began as two friends with a mower and a shovel has grown into a reliable, trusted service focused on quality work and strong client relationships. We both enjoy being our own bosses and take pride in holding each other accountable to the high standards we’ve set. That means when you hire us, you’re getting two people who genuinely care—about the job, about your property, and about earning your trust.

Our business is built on consistency, attention to detail, and communication. Whether it’s a spring cleanup, weekly lawn care, or snow removal after a big storm, we treat every job like it’s our own yard. We’re committed to doing things right the first time and making sure every customer feels looked after.

Sherwood Park is our home, and we’re proud to keep it looking sharp year-round. When you support us, you’re supporting local.
