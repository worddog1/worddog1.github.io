Our Story

Seasonal Soldiers was founded by Luke Boman and Noah Letawsky, two longtime friends who grew up together in Sherwood Park. After graduating high school, they combined their work ethic and passion for outdoor work to start the company three years ago. What began as a small operation has grown into a trusted, locally-owned business known for reliable service, attention to detail, and strong community values.

At Seasonal Soldiers, we take pride in helping our neighbors keep their properties looking their best year-round—whether it's lush lawns in the summer or clear driveways in the winter. We're not just here to get the job done—we're here to do it right.
